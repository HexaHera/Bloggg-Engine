What I’m doing now

(This is a now page, and if you have your own site, you should make one, too.)

Updated June 23rd, 2025, from my home in New Zealand, where a cold and rainy winter has begun.

reading again

Using a Boox Go 6 EPUB reader, I’m reading while exercising, reading in bed in the morning until the house warms up, reading while my kid plays with friends in the forest, and after a long hike when my legs hurt, laying on the couch. Feels great to be excited to read again. Not sure why I wasn’t for a while.

learning Chinese

After three trips to China in one year, I’m enamored. I love being there, and want to live in Shanghai ASAP. I’m spending hours a day learning Chinese using Skritter, Wenlin, Anki, Michel Thomas, Pimsleur, and more under the advice of Olle Linge from Hacking Chinese. It will take years to get to fluency, but I’m committed. I’ve memorized how to read and write (and type!) these 504 characters and components:

Mac

I prefer OpenBSD or at least Linux but typing Chinese is so much better on a Mac that I reluctantly swapped my 10-year-old trusty desktop for the cheapest Mac Mini M4. I miss the Ratpoison window manager the most, but it’s nice to be able to use apps like WeChat, WhatsApp, Wenlin, MacWhisper, OpenMTP and Anki that were impossible or difficult on BSD/Linux.

knee-focused exercise

After tearing the cartilage in my knee last year, cycling improved it enough to exercise again. So I’m working with ATG (“knees over toes”) to re-strengthen.

boy in high school

My son is in his first year of high school, and is so wonderfully insightful, philosophical, talkative, and creative. We just talk talk talk talk like 20+ hours a week, often while walking through forests.

rats are wonderful

The pet rats are adorable little hand-warmers.
