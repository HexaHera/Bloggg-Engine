Presentations → Anything You Want cartoons

Length: 16 minutes. Date recorded: 2011-07

Philosophies on life and business, 7 chapters from my book, turned into short little animations.

Besides books, I'm a fan of movies, comic books, and animation. I love how the combination of visual and oral storytelling is so gripping.

So when I was releasing my own book in July 2011, I hired a great animator - Marvin Te from Plainly Simple Studios - and we worked together to make these 7 videos.

I hope to keep doing new videos like this for future essays, too. I really love the format.
